Our Mission

WeighSaver was created to fight fraud and tampering in weighing machines. Our goal is to ensure fair trade by making sure that customers always know the **true weight**.

How It Works

Each device is assigned a **unique Device ID** that stores its history, performance logs, and tampering alerts. This helps businesses stay transparent and build trust with their customers.
