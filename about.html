About Our Tamper Detection System

What is Tampering?

Tampering refers to any unauthorized attempt to interfere with a machine’s normal operation. Common methods include:

  • 🔌 Circuit Change / Bypass
  • 🧲 Magnetic Interference
  • 💻 Firmware Modification
  • 🔒 Mechanical Seal Removal

Why Tamper Detection Matters

Tampering leads to revenue loss, inaccurate measurements, potential equipment damage, and safety hazards. Detecting tampering ensures transparency and trust in operations.

How We Detect Tampering

  • 🧲 Magnetic sensors detect external magnets
  • ⚡ Circuit monitoring identifies abnormal current flow
  • 🔐 Firmware checksum validation prevents code modification
  • 🔍 Optical/mechanical sensors detect broken seals
  • 📷 Camera & log evidence supports verification
  • 📊 Smart analytics catch unusual consumption patterns

Our Goal

We aim to ensure accuracy, transparency, and security by proactively monitoring and preventing tampering activities.
